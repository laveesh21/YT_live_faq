# Live Coding Stream – FAQ

Welcome! This stream is focused on building real projects live.  
Here are some common questions answered clearly:

---

## 0. What’s the goal of this stream?
The main goal of this stream is to stay consistent with my work and keep improving as a developer.
At the same time, I want to build a positive community where people can hang out, share ideas, and support each other while I work on real projects live.

---

## 1. Do you use AI ?
Yes, I use AI tools the same way developers use documentation, google or any other resources as a way to learn and move faster. 
It helps me quickly understand unfamiliar concepts and explore areas where I’m completely new. I don’t rely on them blindly.

---

## 2. What tech stack do you work on?
I mainly work with a JavaScript-based stack along with databases like MongoDB or MySQL.
At the same time I enjoy exploring other technologies, diving deeper into projects, and improving my understanding of system design and how real-world systems are built.

---

## 3. Is this stream meant for teaching?
Not exactly. This stream is mainly me working live on real projects.  
You’re welcome to watch and pick things up along the way, but it’s not structured like a tutorial or course.

---

## 4. Can beginners follow along?
Yes, you’re welcome to watch!  
Just keep in mind that things may move quickly at times. Basic programming knowledge will help you follow better.

---

## 5. Why do you sometimes search for things or take pauses?
That's normal for a dev to stuck and then try to understand thing and move on.
No one memorizes everything knowing how to find and apply solutions is an important skill.

---

## 6. Are you an expert in computer science?
I’m still learning and improving every day. I’m somewhere in between neither a beginner nor a expert.  
I’m actively learning while building, which is how most real world development works.

---

## 7. Can I correct you if something is wrong?
Yes, respectful and constructive corrections are always welcome.  
Just explain your point clearly.

---

## Final Note
This is a live development workspace.  
You’re welcome to watch, learn, or just hang out. Just keep things respectful and constructive.
